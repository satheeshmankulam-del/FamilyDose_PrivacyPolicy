Overview

🔒 FamilyDose does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device — always.

FamilyDose is a free, offline-first medication tracking app designed for families and caregivers. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled within the app.

2

Data Collection

FamilyDose does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information entered into the app is stored exclusively on your device using secure local on-device storage.

  • No data is sent to any server or cloud service
  • No data is shared with any third party
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No account or sign-up is required
  • No internet connection is needed to use the app
3

Health Data

The app allows users to log health-related information including medication names, dosages, schedules, dose history, and family member names. This data is classified as sensitive health information and is handled as follows:

  • Stored only on your device using local storage
  • Never accessed or viewed by the developer
  • Never transmitted to any server or cloud
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Never used for advertising or analytics
4

Data Retention

All data is retained on your device for as long as the app is installed. The developer does not retain any user data at any point — because no data is ever received by the developer.

Your data exists only on your phone. It is yours entirely.

5

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all app data at any time using either of the following methods:

  • Inside the app: Settings → Clear All Data
  • Uninstall the app from your device

Both actions permanently and irreversibly remove all data stored by FamilyDose on your device. This action cannot be undone.

6

App Permissions

FamilyDose requests the following device permissions. No permission is used to collect or transmit data.

Notifications
To deliver medication dose reminders at scheduled times
Exact Alarm
For precise, on-time reminder delivery for medication schedules
Storage
Only for local data backup export and import — no cloud access
7

Third-Party Services

FamilyDose does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or data brokers.

There are no third parties involved in the operation of this app.

8

Children's Privacy

FamilyDose does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, this app is safe for all ages.
