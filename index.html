Privacy Policy

FamilyDose — Medicine Log App

Last updated: March 2026

FamilyDose is a free, offline medication tracking app built for families and caregivers. This policy explains how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection

FamilyDose does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information — including medication names, dosages, schedules, dose history, and family member names — is stored exclusively on your device using local on-device storage.

No data is ever sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

2. Health Data

The app allows users to log health-related information including medication names, dosages, schedules, and dose history. This data is classified as health information and is handled as follows:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never accessed by the developer
  • Never transmitted or shared with any third party
  • Never uploaded to any cloud or server

3. Data Retention

All data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. The developer does not retain any user data at any point — because no data is ever received.

4. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all app data at any time using either of these methods:

  • Inside the app: Settings → Clear All Data
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

Both actions permanently and irreversibly remove all data stored by FamilyDose on your device.

5. Permissions

FamilyDose requests the following device permissions:

  • Notifications — to deliver medication dose reminders
  • Exact Alarm — for precise, on-time reminder delivery
  • Storage — only for local backup export and import

No permission is used to collect, transmit, or share any data.

6. Third-Party Services

FamilyDose does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. There are no third parties involved.

7. Children's Privacy

FamilyDose does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated date.
