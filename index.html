Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

FamilyDose: Medicine Log App ("the App") is developed by Satheesh. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

FamilyDose does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter in the App — including medicine names, dosages, schedules, photos, and notes — is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All information is saved in your device's local storage. No data is uploaded to any cloud service, database, or third-party server. Your data remains entirely under your control.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. The App does not contain ads.

Internet Access

The App works fully offline. No internet connection is required to use any feature of the App.

Photos and Camera

If you choose to take a photo of a medicine, the image is stored locally on your device within the App's data. Photos are never uploaded or shared externally.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to any third party for any reason.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from the App's Settings page using the "Clear All Data" option. Uninstalling the App also removes all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App stores all data locally and does not transmit any data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
